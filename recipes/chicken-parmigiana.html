Chicken Parmigiana



Description

A hearty and delicious dinner

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts/thighs
  • Flour
  • Bread crumbs (panko preferred)
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Let your chicken defrost, then tenderise and flatten the pieces.
  2. Set up your breading station. Season the flour with salt and pepper.
  3. Dip your chicken into flour, egg mix, then bread crumbs.
  4. Deep fry the chicken.
  5. Boil pasta according to box instructions.
  6. Crush tomatoes and put into a pan with oil.
  7. Let reduce, add tomato paste/ketchup
  8. Add pasta water
  9. Add garlic powder, oregano, basil towards the end.
  10. spoon some sauce over the chicken, then add Mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  11. Put chicken in the oven just until the cheese melts.
