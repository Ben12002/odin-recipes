Steak


Description

A very simple and easy, but delicious steak recipe.

Ingredients

  • Steak, any cut, about 250g
  • Butter
  • Thyme (dried/fresh)
  • Rosemary (dried/fresh)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Garlic
  • Shallots

Steps

  1. Heat up a cast iron/stainless pan until very hot.
  2. Add a generous amount of high smoke point oil.
  3. Season your steak generously with salt and pepper.
  4. Once oil heats up, add in your steak after patting it dry.
  5. Do not move the steak, let one side cook for a few minutes.
  6. Throw in your thyme and rosemary.
  7. Flip the steak, then throw in your garlic and shallots.
  8. About a minute before the steak is done, throw in some butter.
  9. Let the butter melt, then spoon it over the steak to baste it.
  10. Once the butter starts browning, lift the steak from the pan onto a plate.
  11. Pour the liquids in the pan over the steak, and let it rest for ~10 minutes.
